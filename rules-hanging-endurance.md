## ğŸ’¡ Strategy Tips

### General Tips
1. **Stay centered** - Start in the middle of the zone, not the edges
2. **Small movements** - Micro-adjustments are better than big swings
3. **Anticipate shrinking** - Move inward as the zone shrinks
4. **Breathe steadily** - Calm hands make precise movements
5. **Watch for patterns** - Wind gusts have a rhythm

### Advanced Techniques
1. **Pre-position** - Slightly favor the center before gusts
2. **Quick recovery** - After a gust, immediately re-center
3. **Grip management** - Brief zone exits are okay if you recover quickly
4. **Zone awareness** - Keep peripheral vision on zone borders

### Mental Game
1. **Stay calm** - Panic leads to overcorrection
2. **Focus on YOUR arena** - Don't get distracted by opponent
3. **Ignore falls** - Your partner falling shouldn't affect you
4. **Endurance mindset** - It's a marathon, not a sprint
