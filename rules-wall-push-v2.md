## ğŸ¯ Objective

Push the three blocks toward the **opponent's side** of the arena. When time expires, each block's position determines who scores:

- Block on **RED's side** (left of center) = **1 point for BLUE**
- Block on **BLUE's side** (right of center) = **1 point for RED**
- Block **exactly on center** = No points awarded

**The team with the most points wins!**

### How to Generate Push Power
1. Each pair of players is assigned **two keys**
2. Players must **alternate pressing** their two keys as fast as possible
3. Simply holding or spamming one key will NOT generate power
4. The faster you alternate, the more push force you generate!
