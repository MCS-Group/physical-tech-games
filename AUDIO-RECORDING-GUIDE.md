## üéôÔ∏è Recording Tips

### Voice Style:
- **Game Intros**: Energetic sports announcer voice
- **Victories**: Triumphant, celebratory tone
- **Championships**: Epic, over-the-top excitement
- **Countdown**: Clear, rhythmic timing

### Technical Settings:
- **Format**: MP3
- **Bitrate**: 128kbps or higher
- **Sample Rate**: 44.1kHz
- **Channels**: Mono or Stereo (Mono is fine for voice)
- **Normalize audio**: Make sure all files have similar volume levels

### Recording Environment:
- Use a quiet room (no echo)
- Get close to microphone (but not too close)
- Remove background noise in editing
- Add slight reverb/room tone for "arena" effect (optional)

## ‚úÖ Testing Your Audio

After recording, test the audio:

1. **Place files** in `assets/audio/` folder
2. **Open the game** in Chrome
3. **Play a match** and listen for:
   - Game intro voice-over at start
   - Countdown "3 2 1 GO"
   - Victory announcement after match
   - Championship announcement after final

**Note:** If a file is missing or has wrong name, the game will:
- Show a warning in console (F12)
- Use procedural beep sounds as fallback
- Continue working normally
